Comfort & Etiquette

Clear, comfortable, chaperoned space

The room is arranged so participants can engage sincerely while feeling safe, respected, and unpressured.

What to expect

The environment is intentionally structured. Separate seating, visible hosts, and clear etiquette help everyone understand what is appropriate before conversations begin.

  • Separate seating and open social areas are available.
  • Hosts and chaperones remain visible and approachable.
  • Conversation expectations are explained so nobody has to guess.
