Step 1

Welcome by the imam and hosts

The evening begins with clarity, adab, and a calm explanation of the purpose of the gathering.

What to expect

Participants are greeted by the imam and trained community hosts. The opening sets the tone: sincere intention, respectful conversation, privacy, and no pressure.

  • A short reminder about halal intention and good manners.
  • A walkthrough of the evening format and room layout.
  • Clear guidance on introductions, boundaries, and follow-up requests.
